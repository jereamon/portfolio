About me:

After multiple introductions to programming over the years I finally decided to get serious. In August of 2017 I began working my way through every resource I could find regarding Python. After a few months of this I was feeling confident in my basic abilities and decided to shift my focus.


This was when I decided to take the leap into web development. I switched from Python to HTML and CSS and after I had a firm grasp on both of those moved into Javascript. The skills I had drilled home with Python paid off when picking up another programming language and I quickly began learning how to manipulate the DOM and add additional functionality to my web pages.


I'm currently building as many pages as I can to try to find the most efficient and effective ways to develop sites from scratch as well as create scallable, easy to understand code.
